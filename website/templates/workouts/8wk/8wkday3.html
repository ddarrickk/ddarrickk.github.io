Day 5

Equipment necessary: Dumbbells OR Barbell with plates, flat surface to lay on, Full Gym

Exercise 1: DB Overhead Press

3 SETS (12, 10, 8 REPS) 45-60 SECONDS REST

1. Grab a pair of dumbbells, and lift them up to the starting position at your shoulders.

2. Inhale and lightly brace your core. Press the dumbbells up to straight arms, while exhaling.

3. Inhale at the top, or while lowering the dumbbells with control back to your shoulders.

4. Repeat for reps.

Exercise 2: Dumbbell Fly

3 SETS (12, 10, 8 REPS) 45-60 SECONDS REST

1. Start by lying down on a flat bench. Hold a dumbbell in each hand, resting the weight on the top of your thighs. Your palms will be facing each other.

2. Lift the dumbbells one at at time. Hold the dumbbells in front of you, shoulder width apart.

3. Bend your elbows slightly and lower your arms at both sides using a wide arc.

4. Bring your arms back to the starting position, using the same arc of motion as you did to lower the weights.

5. Repeat for reps.

Exercise 3: Overhead Press

3 SETS (12, 10, 8 REPS) 45-60 SECONDS REST

1. Stand with feet shoulder-width apart and hold the dumbbells/barbell at shoulder height with your elbows at a 90-degree angle.

2. Slowly lift the dumbbells/barbell above your head without fully straightening your arms. Pause at the top.

3. Slowly return to the start position.

4. Repeat for reps.

Exercise 4: Lateral Raise

3 SETS (12, 10, 8 REPS) 45-60 SECONDS REST

1. Start with the dumbbells resting on the side of the thigh. The arms should be slightly bent.

2. Using your shoulder, lift the arm in a semi-circular motion until slightly above parallel.

3. Return to the starting position slowly while maintaining a tight core.

4. Repeat for reps.

Exercise 5: Dumbbell Shrug

3 SETS (12, 10, 8 REPS) 45-60 SECONDS REST

1. Stand up straight with a dumbbell in each hand. Your palms should be facing your torso, with your arms extended to your sides.

2. Raise the dumbbells by lifting your shoulders as high as possible, exhaling as you do so. Hold the contraction at the top for a second.

3. Bring the dumbbells back to the starting position.

4. Repeat for reps.

Exercise 6: Cable Upright Row

3 SETS (12, 10, 8 REPS) 45-60 SECONDS REST

1. Place the cable pulley in the very bottom notch of the machine.

2. Grab onto the bar with both hands, palms facing down and in towards your body.

3. Bending at the elbows pull the bar up to the top of your chest.

4. Repeat for reps.

Exercise 7: Wrist Curl

3 SETS (12, 10, 8 REPS) 45-60 SECONDS REST

1. Exhale as you slowly close your hands and curl the dumbbells upward by flexing your wrists.

2. Hold for a count of two.

3. Inhale as you lower the dumbbells to the starting position and open your hands a little to allow the dumbbells to once again roll towards your fingers.

4. Repeat for reps.

Exercise 8: Reverse Wrist Curl

3 SETS (12, 10, 8 REPS) 45-60 SECONDS REST

1. Face palms downward.

2. Exhale as you slowly close your hands and curl the dumbbells upward by flexing your wrists.

3. Hold for a count of two.

4. Inhale as you lower the dumbbells to the starting position and open your hands a little to allow the dumbbells to once again roll towards your fingers.

5. Repeat for reps.
