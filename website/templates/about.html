Strength

\ ˈstreŋ(k)th, ˈstren(t)th \

I : force as measured in numbers : effective numbers of any body or organization

The first goal of Strength is to build a community that welcomes people from all walks of life to build a community focused on one thing, getting stonger.

II : the quality or state of being strong : capacity for exertion or endurance

The second goal of Strength is to offer quality content with a variety of different ways to develop stronger bodies.

III : one regarded as embodying or affording force or firmness

The third goal of Strength is the development of users. Strength is not simply defined as the amount of weight a person can lift. It is also important to embody what strength means while developing a strong personality.

IV : power to resist force : SOLIDITY, TOUGHNESS

The fourth goal of Strength is the empowerment. Our users will be nurtured in a way that allows them to surpass what they believe is possible.

V : power of resisting attack : IMPREGNABILITY

The fifth goal of Strength is to prepare our users for life. After going through the Strength program, our users will be able to face any challenge and become unconquerable.
