I : Obliques

Side Planks

1. Start on your side with your feet stacked on top of each other and your bottom forearm directly below your shoulder.

2. Engage your core and raise your hips until your body is in a straight line from head to toe.

3. Hold this exact position without letting your hips drop for 30 seconds or more, then repeat on the other side.

Sit-Ups to Twist

1. Lie on your back with your knees bent and feet flat to the floor.

2. Place your hands behind your head, engage your core and do a full sit-up. At the top of the sit-up, bring your right elbow to your left knee and twist your body toward that side.

3. Lower back down to start.

4. Repeat this movement alternating sides each time.

Spidermans

1. Start in a high plank, arms extended and your hands under your shoulders, with your core engaged.

2. Bring your left knee to your left elbow. Use your upper body to turn toward your knee to perform what looks like a side crunch.

3. Continue the movement, alternating sides.

II : Upper Abs

Hollow Hold

1. Lay on your back with arms extended overhead and legs pressed together.

2. Lift your legs and upper torso off the floor. Hold this position.

3. To perform the hollow rock, simply rock back and forth in this position, minimizing movement at the hip and shoulder joints.

Ab Rollout

1. Get on your knees and grip your equipment of choice with hands shoulder-width apart.

2. Extend your hips towards the floor and let your chest sink forward toward the ground without overarching your lower back.

3. Squeeze the lat muscles and pull yourself back to the starting position.

Dragonfly

1. Lie on your back and hold a sturdy pole, column, or bench behind you.

2. Lift your hips as you roll your weight on your shoulders. Then lift your legs and torso into a straight line, keeping your weight on your shoulders and upper back.

3. Slowly lower your legs towards the floor until theyâ€™re parallel, keeping your core and glutes engaged.

4. Pause for a few seconds and return to the starting position.

III : Lower Abs

Heel tap

1. Lie faceup with your arms to your sides and hands against the floor. Bend your knees, keeping your calves parallel to the floor.

2. Slowly lower your flexed feet forward until your heels barely touch the floor.

3. Squeeze your abs to help raise your feet back up to the starting position.

Straight leg raise

1. Lie faceup with your arms to your sides and hands against the floor.

2. Brace your core and lift your straight legs slowly off the floor, bringing them to 90 degrees.

3. Slowly lower your legs back to the floor.

Flutter kick

1. Lie faceup with your legs extended, toes pointed, and hands tucked under your glutes to support your lower back.

2. Lift both legs a few inches off the floor.

3. Alternately kick your legs up and down.
