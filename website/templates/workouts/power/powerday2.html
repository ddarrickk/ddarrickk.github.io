Day 2

Equipment necessary: Dumbbells OR Barbell with plates, flat surface to lay on, Full Gym

Exercise 1 : Squat

5 SETS 5 REPS 60-90 SECOND REST

1. Load a barbell on a rack at upper-chest height.

2. Step under the barbell so that it rests on the back of your shoulders, and grasp the bar at each side.

3. Dismount the barbell and step backward carefully. Your feet should be shoulder-width apart and pointing slightly outwards.

4. Inhale as you squat down by simultaneously pushing your butt backward and bending your knees forward. Keep your torso upright, and descend at least until your knees are bent at a 90-degree angle.

5. Exhale as you push your body back up to the starting position, driving through your heels and keeping your torso upright.

6. Repeat for reps.

Exercise 2 : Overhead Press

5 SETS 5 REPS 60-90 SECOND REST

1. Stand with feet shoulder-width apart and hold the dumbbells/barbell at shoulder height with your elbows at a 90-degree angle.

2. Slowly lift the dumbbells/barbell above your head without fully straightening your arms. Pause at the top.

3. Slowly return to the start position.

4. Repeat for reps.

Exercise 3 : Deadlift

1 SETS 5 REPS 60-90 SECOND REST

1. Stand with your mid-foot under the barbell

2. Bend over and grab the bar with a shoulder-width grip

3. Bend your knees until your shins touch the bar

4. Lift your chest up and straighten your lower back

5. Take a big breath, hold it, and stand up with the weight

Exercise 4: Dumbbell Curls

1 SETS 5 REPS 60-90 SECOND REST

1. Standing with a dumbbell in each hand and elbows touching the side of the torso.

2. Holding the upper arm stationary, curl the right weight with palm facing forward. Lift the dumbbell toward the shoulder until the bicep is fully contracted. Hold this position for a second and squeeze the bicep.

3. Lower the right dumbbell to the starting position and repeat the same movement with the left dumbbell.

Exercise 6 : Leg Curls

1 SETS 5 REPS 60-90 SECOND REST

1. Lie down on a leg curl machine with the pad on the back of your legs. It should be positioned a few inches under your calves.

2. Stretch your legs out fully and grasp the side handles of the machine, keeping your torso flat on the bench as you do so. This is the starting position.

3. Curl your lower legs up as far as you can while keeping your upper legs on the pad. Once your legs are fully contracted, hold the position for a second.

4. Bring your legs back to the starting position, inhaling as you do so. Repeat for the number of reps in your set.
