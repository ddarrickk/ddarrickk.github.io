Barbell Glute Bridge

1. Load a barbell with rubber bumper plates (if possible), as you want the bar higher up off of the ground. Now, roll the barbell towards you, so itâ€™s sitting on your hips.

2. Place your upper back on a bench and bend your knees with your feet flat on the floor.

3. Squeeze your glutes and drive your hips up, holding onto the barbell with both hands to prevent it from falling until your body is in a straight line from knee to chin.

4. Hold this position for a beat and then lower the weight back down.

5. Repeat for reps.

Belt Squat

1. Set the belt around your hip, brace your core, and lift the weight.

2. Release the weight pin and get yourself into position.

3. With your chest up, squat down until the bottoms of your thighs are parallel to the floor.

4. Now, drive back up by pushing your feet through the platform.

5. Repeat for reps.

Rear Foot Elevated Split Squat

1. Stand with a dumbbell in each hand, and take a step forward, placing one foot, toes down, on an elevated surface behind you.

2. It should be low enough to not shift your hips to one side. The distance of your step should be about one foot.

3. Keep your chest up and squat down until both of your legs bend to around 90 degrees.

4. Stand back up by driving your front foot through the floor.

5. Repeat for reps.
