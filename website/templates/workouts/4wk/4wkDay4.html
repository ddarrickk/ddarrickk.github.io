Day 5

Equipment necessary: Dumbbells OR Barbell with plates, flat surface to lay on, Full Gym

Exercise 1 : Squat

5 SETS (12, 10, 8, 6, 12 REPS) 60 SECONDS REST

1. Load a barbell on a rack at upper-chest height.

2. Step under the barbell so that it rests on the back of your shoulders, and grasp the bar at each side.

3. Dismount the barbell and step backward carefully. Your feet should be shoulder-width apart and pointing slightly outwards.

4. Inhale as you squat down by simultaneously pushing your butt backward and bending your knees forward. Keep your torso upright, and descend at least until your knees are bent at a 90-degree angle.

5. Exhale as you push your body back up to the starting position, driving through your heels and keeping your torso upright.

6. Repeat for reps.

Exercise 2 : Deadlift

5 SETS (12, 10, 8, 6, 12 REPS) 60 SECONDS REST

1. Stand with your mid-foot under the barbell

2. Bend over and grab the bar with a shoulder-width grip

3. Bend your knees until your shins touch the bar

4. Lift your chest up and straighten your lower back

5. Take a big breath, hold it, and stand up with the weight

Exercise 3 : Leg Extensions

5 SETS (12, 10, 8, 6, 12 REPS) 60 SECONDS REST

1. Sit on a leg extension machine. Position your legs under the pad and grasp the side bars with your hands. This is the starting position.

2. Extend your legs to the maximum, exhaling as you do so. Pause a second in this contracted position.

3. Lower the weight back to the original position as you inhale. Make sure your legs don't go past the 90-degree angle point.

4. Repeat for reps.

Exercise 4 : Leg Curls

5 SETS (12, 10, 8, 6, 12 REPS) 60 SECONDS REST

1. Lie down on a leg curl machine with the pad on the back of your legs. It should be positioned a few inches under your calves.

2. Stretch your legs out fully and grasp the side handles of the machine, keeping your torso flat on the bench as you do so. This is the starting position.

3. Curl your lower legs up as far as you can while keeping your upper legs on the pad. Once your legs are fully contracted, hold the position for a second.

4. Bring your legs back to the starting position, inhaling as you do so. Repeat for the number of reps in your set.

Exercise 5 : Calf Raise

5 SETS (12, 10, 8, 6, 12 REPS) 60 SECONDS REST

1. Sit on a calf raise machine and place your toes on the foot platform. You heels should hang off the back of the platform.

2. Adjust the thigh padding so your lower thighs rest just under the padding.

3. Grasp the handle bars and then raise up on your toes to release the safety for your starting position.

4. Inhale as you slowly lower your heels until they are below your toes and your calves are fully stretched.

5. Exhale as you raise your heels as high as you can until you are on your tippy toes.

6. Repeat for reps.

Exercise 6 : Leg Raise

5 SETS (12, 10, 8, 6, 12 REPS) 60 SECONDS REST

1. Lay down with your back flat on the floor, feet straight out and together and your hands on your side.

2. While keep your legs as straight as you can lift your feet up in the air between 6-12 inches.

3. You can either hold for designated period of time or you can raise them up and then lower them right back down.

4. Repeat for reps.

Exercise 7 : Decline Sit Up

5 SETS (12, 10, 8, 6, 12 REPS) 60 SECONDS REST

1. Sit on the bench with bent knees and your feet under the padded bar.

2. Cross your arms over your chest, or interlace your fingers around the base of your skull.

3. Lift your torso up to bring your chest to your thighs.

4. Repeat for reps.

Exercise 8 : Bench Side Bend

4 SETS (12, 10, 8, 6 REPS) 45-60 SECONDS REST

1. Lie on your left side, right arm on a right hip or extended, place your left forearm on a bench and prop yourself up.

2. Raise your hips so your body is straight from ankles to head.

3. Repeat for reps.
