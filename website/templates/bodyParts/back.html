I : Lats

Cable Lat Pulldown

1. Sit on the pull down machine with knee pads adjusted to provide support, preventing your body from being pulled out of position.

2. Grip the pull down bar with a pronated grip slightly outside of shoulder width.

3. As you exhale, pull the bar down until it touches the upper chest.

4. Pause and squeeze the back muscles.

5. Slowly bring the bar back to the starting position with the lats fully stretched.

6. Repeat for reps.

DB Row

1. Kneel over side of bench by placing knee and hand of supporting arm on bench.

2. Grab the dumbbell from the floor while keeping a straight back.

3. Flexing the core, keep head in neutral positon and pull the dumbbell towards your stomach.

4. Squeeze your back as you breath out at the top position.

5. Lower the dumbbell to the starting position.

6. Repeat for reps.

Seated Cable Row

1. Grip the handle attachment with both hands and sit with chest upright and back straight.

2. With shoulders retracted, pull the handle towards the mid torso.

3. Squeeze the back muscles as the handle approaches the torso and hold before returning to the starting position.

4. Repeat for reps.

II : Rhomboids

Face Pulls

1. Secure band to object at head level and hold handles with arms extended in front.

2. Keeping elbows at shoulder height, pull handles towards the side of the head while exhaling.

3. Hold and return to starting position while exhaling.

4. Repeat for reps.

DB Reverse Flys

1. Adjust the bench so that it's at a 30 to 45 degree angle and lie down on the bench with your chest and torso pressing into the bench.

2. Hold a dumbbell on each hand and let your arms hang down with a slight bend at your elbows. This is your starting position.

3. Squeeze your shoulder blades together and move the weights out and away from each other to the side in an arc motion while exhaling.

4. Pause at the top when your arms are parallel to the floor, then return to the starting position.

5. Repeat for reps.

BB Rows

1. Holding the barbell with a pronated grip, bend the legs and tilt the torso with a straight back.

2. Flexing the core, keep head in neutral position, pull the barbell towards your stomach.

3. Squeeze your back as you breath, return to the starting position.

4. Inhale and lower the barbell to the starting position.

5. Repeat for reps.

III : Traps

DB Shrugs

1. Holding a dumbbell in each hand, stand with feet shoulder width apart, back straight and core braced.

2. While exhaling, contract the trap muscles and elevate the dumbbells.

3. Hold at the upper position and lower back to the starting position while exhaling.

4. Repeat for reps.

DB Lateral Raise

1. Start with the dumbbells resting on the side of the thigh. The arms should be slightly bent.

2. Using your shoulder, lift the arm in a semi-circular motion until slightly above parallel.

3. Return to the starting position slowly while maintaining a tight core.

4. Repeat for reps.

DB Upright Row

1. Hold dumbbells with arms at shoulder width and grip with a pronated girp.

2. Use shoulders to lift the dumbbells as you exhale.

3. Elbows should lead movement and our hands should be close to the body as it is elevated.

4. Continue to lift until hands are at your chin level. Hold at this position as you contract the shoulder muscles.

5. Lower to starting position.

6. Repeat for reps.
