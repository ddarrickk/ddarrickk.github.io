Day 3

Equipment necessary: Dumbbells OR Barbell with plates, flat surface to lay on, Full Gym

Exercise 1: Deadlift

3 SETS (12, 10, 8 REPS) 45-60 SECONDS REST

1. Stand up tall with one dumbbell in each hand, palms facing you.

2. Engage your abs, and hinge your hips back as you slowly lower the dumbbells toward the middle of your shins.

3. Stand back up, then squeeze your glutes at the top.

Exercise 2: Chin Ups

3 SETS (12, 10, 8 REPS) 45-60 SECONDS REST

1. Place hands on the bar with palms facing yourself and a grip slightly within shoulder width.

2. Retract the shoulders and tuck them down and keep the core tight and back slightly arched

3. Breathng out, pull the torso upward until the head is above the bar keeping elbows against the side of the body.

4. Lower back to the starting position while breathing in.

5. Repeat for reps.

Exercise 3: Rows

3 SETS (12, 10, 8 REPS) 45-60 SECONDS REST

1. Holding the barbell with a pronated grip, bend the legs and tilt the torso with a straight back.

2. Flexing the core, keep head in neutral position, pull the barbell towards your stomach.

3. Squeeze your back as you breath, return to the starting position.

4. Inhale and lower the barbell to the starting position.

5. Repeat for reps.

Exercise 4: Seated Cable Row

3 SETS (12, 10, 8 REPS) 45-60 SECONDS REST

1. Grip the handle attachment with both hands and sit with chest upright and back straight.

2. With shoulders retracted, pull the handle towards the mid torso.

3. Squeeze the back muscles as the handle approaches the torso and hold before returning to the starting position.

4. Repeat for reps.

Exercise 5: Lat Pulldown

3 SETS (12, 10, 8 REPS) 45-60 SECONDS REST

1. Sit on the pull down machine with knee pads adjusted to provide support, preventing your body from being pulled out of position.

2. Grip the pull down bar with a pronated grip slightly outside of shoulder width.

3. As you exhale, pull the bar down until it touches the upper chest.

4. Pause and squeeze the back muscles.

5. Slowly bring the bar back to the starting position with the lats fully stretched.

6. Repeat for reps.

Exercise 6: Concentration Curl

3 SETS (12, 10, 8 REPS) 45-60 SECONDS REST

1. Sit down on a bench holding a dumbbell in your left hand, and rest your elbow on the inside of your left thigh.

2. Squeeze the left biceps and bring the dumbbell toward your chest.

3. Lower the left arm to the starting position.

4. Repeat for reps.

Exercise 7: Barbell Curl

3 SETS (12, 10, 8 REPS) 45-60 SECONDS REST

1. Stand up straight while holding a barbell in a shoulder-width grip.

2. Contract your biceps to curl the weight forward. Your upper arms should remain stationary during this process.

3. Continue moving the barbell until the biceps are fully contracted and the bar is at shoulder height. Hold this position for a second and then squeeze your biceps.

4. Bring the barbell back to the starting position.

5. Repeat for reps.

Exercise 8: Z-Bar Preacher Curl

3 SETS (12, 10, 8 REPS) 45-60 SECONDS REST

1. Grab an Z-bar with an underhand grip. Position hands at the first grip portion from the center.

2. Sit down on a preacher bench with your upper arms(triceps) resting on the incline pad. Hold bar in front of your face with palms facing you.

3. Lower the bar down without moving your upper arms, only allowing your forearms to move.

4. Curl weight back up to starting position.

Exercise 9: Incline Dumbbell Curl

3 SETS (12, 10, 8 REPS) 45-60 SECONDS REST

1.Position an incline bench at roughly 55-65 degrees, select the desired weight from the rack, and sit upright with your back flat against the pad.

2. Using a supinated (palms up) grip, take a deep breath and curl both dumbbells towards your shoulders.

3. Once the biceps are fully shortened, slowly lower the weights back to the starting position.

4. Repeat for reps.
